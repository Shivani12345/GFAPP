Know The Law

The Ghanaian law regulating these crimes is the 2005 Human Trafficking Act (2005).

The Act has a clear definition of human trafficking:

    the recruitment, transportation, transfer, harbouring, trading or receipt of persons for the purpose of exploitation within and across national borders by:

  • (a) The use of threats, force or other forms of coercion, abduction, fraud, deception, the abuse of power or exploitation of vulnerability, or
  • (b) Giving or receiving payments and benefits to achieve consent
