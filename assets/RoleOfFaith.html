Create a safe, nonjudgmental place that encourages trafficked individuals to seek assistance.

  • Offer Support and Referral Services: Offer emotional, spiritual, and material support to victims of human trafficking as well as information on local resources.
  • Safety Plan: Assist victims to develop a safe exit strategy and a safety plan using in-house and/or local resources.
  • Call: Contact a Human Trafficking Hotline to report suspected trafficking and for information on local and national resources.
