How to talk to your congregations

Begin by introducing the topic of modern slavery and human trafficking. Don’t assume that your congregation will have heard about it before. Use some key figures, like the ones here. Explain how your country is affected by this (number of victims, or industries affected, or imported goods), using the regional report available here

Make it relatable by using one of the case studies mentioned here

You can use the tips found here to prepare your talk.

Now that you’ve introduced the problem/issue to your congregation, it’s time to cover what your faith/religion says about it.

  • For Catholic/Christian communities, use the resources available here and here
  • For Muslim communities, you can find some resources here and here.
  • We suggest you focus on the Quran chapter 90, verses 12-13, which address slavery.
  • For Buddhist communities, you can find some resources here.
  • For Jewish communities, you can find some resources here.
  • For Hindu communities, you can find some resources here.

What’s next?

You can also create permanent resources or material dealing with modern slavery, for people to take home or consult on their own. You can find some ideas here.
